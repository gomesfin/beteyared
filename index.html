Who We Are

Melake Selam Kesis (Priest) Yared Gebremedhin is the head of the Debre Selam Kidus Gabriel Orthodox Tewahedo Church in Columbus, Ohio. Having founded and led the church for 16 years, Kesis Yared holds a PhD in Christian Counseling from Northwestern Christian University. He has guided myriads of youth into becoming responsible Christian adults. Bete Yared strengthens youth through biblical education and Christian counseling.
